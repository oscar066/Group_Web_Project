Accommodation

The Mikasa 146 luxury guest rooms and 100 suites have been refurbished throughout the years to maintain their classic elegance and unique personality. Signature suites honor historical and celebrated world figures with each room themed on influential personalities. Many rooms afford breathtaking elevated views of the tranquil, landscaped tropical gardens.
